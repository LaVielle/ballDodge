Ball Dodge

A super high pressure action game

INFO

Your goal is to last as dodge incoming balls for as long as possible.
Not all balls are created equal:
Red balls increase the speed of the balls coming at you
Brown take points off your score
Blue balls slow down the balls coming at you
Green balls add points to your score
